Full-Frame 3D Vision Automatic Seamless Welding Machine - Product Description

Key Features
  1. Equipped with Shenzhen Chuangxin 2KW continuous fiber laser (domestic best welding laser).
  2. Wuhan Xinghong water-cooled 2KW laser head or dual-swing 3KW laser head.
  3. Domestic No.1 brand: Wuhan Hanli vertical dual-temperature dual-control 2KW water chiller.
  4. 10-inch touchscreen & 15-inch real-time monitoring display, independently developed PLC integrated intelligent control system, automatic memory of each profile data. Full-automatic 3D vision scanning positioning for automatic welding, no manual intervention required, and can achieve any position supplementary welding and pause.
  5. Equipped with Taiwan Yanhua industrial computer & Philips keyboard, mouse; specially customized 3D vision scanning camera.
  6. Uses the most advanced bus servo motor control, extremely fast response speed, high precision, accurate positioning.
  7. High-precision precision ball screw & 6-grade precision ground rack, precision linear guide rail, stable operation, high positioning accuracy.
  8. Equipped with high-precision precision planetary gear reducer wire feeding mechanism & wire frame, servo motor controls wire feeding precisely, smooth, large torque, with automatic compensation & automatic anti-stick anti-pull wire function, beneficial for next precise wire filling, high gap tolerance for assembly.
  9. Laser head automatic focus, can automatically reach focus position for welding, no manual focusing needed, greatly improving efficiency and weld consistency.
  10. Equipped with 4 widened thickened wear-resistant synchronous conveyor belts, just place the outer frame on the conveyor belt for automatic transport, automatic positioning, automatic return to start point after welding for convenient welding on the other side. Conveyor speed stepless variable frequency speed regulation.
  11. Equipped with 5m x 3 x 6 square high flame retardant national standard oxygen-free copper power line, China Chint air switch, AC contactor, Omron relay.
  12. Water chiller no need to press separately to distribution box to avoid complex separate control of welder and water chiller, built-in high power bull plug socket, realizing integrated control of welder and water chiller.
