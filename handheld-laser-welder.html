Handheld Laser Welding Machine - Product Description

Key Features
  1. Ergonomic Design: Lightweight handheld head, comfortable grip, greatly reduces labor intensity.
  2. Safety Design: Touchscreen buttons + mechanical buttons dual interlock protection, safety circuit protection (gas nozzle must contact metal to trigger light), nozzle anti-splash and anti-reflection.
  3. Global Brands: Laser source, water chiller, electronic components, and mechanical structure all use top international or domestic brands.
  4. Stable Welding System: Almost maintenance-free, easy to master for beginners, no welder certificate required.
  5. Uniform Energy Distribution: High-quality light spot for welding characteristics.
  6. Versatile Application: Suitable for various metal weld splicing including stainless steel, carbon steel, iron, galvanized sheet, and aluminum alloy.
