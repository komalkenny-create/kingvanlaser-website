Fully Automatic Robot Welding Workstation - Product Description

Key Features
  1. Working radius 1450mm, hollow design to avoid workpiece interference.
  2. Compact size, high control precision, intelligent self-check.
  3. Small, lightweight, simple and intuitive.
  4. Built-in anti-collision, protects welding gun.
  5. Reliable soft switching inverter technology, ultra-low spatter, full digital control, precise control of droplet transition, small welding deformation, ensures continuous welding quality, intelligent parameter matching.
  6. Built-in encoder feedback, even if external voltage or wire feeding resistance changes, still ensures stable wire feeding.
  7. Flip positioner achieves full-position welding on both sides of the workpiece.
  8. Automatic positioning and clamping based on workpiece shape and size, independently developed design, convenient and efficient, can achieve batch continuous welding.
  9. Real-time communication, precise current and voltage control.
  10. Optional dual-station configuration.
