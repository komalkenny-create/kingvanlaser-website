Tri-Axis Dual-Station Seamless Welding Machine - Product Description

Key Features
  1. Similar to Dual-Axis model but with Tri-Axis motion system for more complex welding paths.
  2. Mainly used for seamless laser welding of aluminum and aluminum-wood doors & windows.
  3. Equipped with a 1500W fiber continuous laser and national patented technology circular swing laser welding gun, automatically produces fish-scale pattern, beautiful and full.
  4. Dual-station design, stations 1 and 2 can weld different materials simultaneously, improving efficiency.
  5. Embedded 10-inch touchscreen control + high-precision servo motor + Taiwan precision screw guide, precise and reliable motion, user-friendly interface.
  6. Automatic memory of profile data, one-key call, intelligent and efficient.
