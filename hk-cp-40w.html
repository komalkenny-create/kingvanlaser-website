HK-CP-40W Aluminum-Wood Window Laser Marking Machine - Product Description

Key Features
  1. The HK-CP-40W CO2 laser marking machine is the most advanced fourth-generation laser marking system. It uses the world's most advanced CO2 laser source with high photoelectric conversion efficiency (up to 70%) and a high-speed scanning galvanometer system for marking.
  2. Air-cooled design with compact size and excellent output beam quality (M2 close to 1, beam divergence angle of 0.24mard). High pulse repetition frequency, stable power, single pulse energy fluctuation less than 1%, enabling high-speed laser marking with precise control of spot size, depth, and shape.
  3. High reliability, capable of engraving non-metal materials and some painted metals, with ultra-long operating life and energy efficiency.
  4. Highly integrated, compact machine structure with low energy consumption.
  5. Fast marking speed, marking efficiency is 5 times that of traditional products.
  6. Long lifespan, maintenance-free, low operating cost. Lifespan can reach 10 times that of traditional products.
