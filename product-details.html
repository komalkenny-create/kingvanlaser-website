Fiber Laser Marking Machine - Product Description

Product Overview

The KingVan Fiber Laser Marking Machine utilizes advanced fiber laser technology to deliver high-precision, permanent marking on a wide range of metals and non-metal materials. Designed for industrial applications, this machine provides reliable, maintenance-free operation with exceptional marking quality.

Key Advantages
  • High Precision: Marking precision up to 0.01mm, ensuring clear and accurate marks even for tiny characters and complex patterns.
  • High Speed: Marking speed up to 7000mm/s, 10x faster than traditional marking methods.
  • Permanent Marking: Marks are resistant to heat, abrasion, and chemicals, ideal for traceability and anti-counterfeiting.
  • Low Operating Cost: No consumables required, air-cooled design, energy-efficient with long laser source lifespan (up to 100,000 hours).
  • Easy Operation: User-friendly EZCAD software support, compatible with AutoCAD, CorelDRAW, Photoshop, and PLT files.
  • Wide Material Compatibility: Suitable for stainless steel, carbon steel, aluminum, copper, brass, titanium, plastic, PVC, ABS, and more.
Typical Applications
  • Hardware tools, knives, measuring instruments
  • Auto parts, bearings, gears, buttons
  • Electronic components, circuit boards, chips
  • Jewelry, watches, eyeglasses, sanitary ware
  • Medical devices, surgical instruments
  • QR codes, barcodes, serial numbers, logos
